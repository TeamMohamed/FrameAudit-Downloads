Eligibility

Who this screened desk is for.

  • You represent a company, firm, or institutional organization with an owned email domain.
  • You can identify the organization name, your role, and the matter category clearly.
  • You need case review, commercial briefing, private software access, or a serious partnership discussion.
  • You can summarize the dispute or requirement without sending evidence through the public page.
  • You understand that qualification happens before any private delivery or transfer route is opened.

Screening desk

Submit a screened institutional inquiry.

Browser screening checks common personal email domains, compares the work email against the organization website you provide, filters obvious automated submissions, and can require a challenge before server-side intake is accepted.

Human verification

If this challenge is active, it must complete before the inquiry can be submitted to the screening endpoint.

Fallback Direct Email

Passing screening opens a structured email draft for manual review. When a server endpoint is configured, rejected domains, rapid-fire submissions, obvious bots, and repeated bad traffic can be filtered before they reach manual review.

Manual screening

How a qualified inquiry moves from first contact to a private channel.

Public contact is just the screening layer. Domain ownership, organizational fit, matter type, and urgency are checked before any private exchange path is opened.

1

Organization and domain checked

The inquiry is reviewed for a real organization, owned domain, and credible institutional fit.

2

Scope and urgency screened

The matter type, commercial path, or software-access request is checked before a response path is chosen.

3

Reply route selected

Qualified senders are answered directly with the right next step: review intake, commercial briefing, or software discussion.

4

Private channel opened

If the matter is accepted, instructions for private document or evidence transfer are provided outside the public site.

5

Engagement or access confirmed

Quote, delivery window, or private-access path is confirmed only after the screening stage is cleared.
