Confidential case intake

Open confidential review for contested footage.

FrameAudit is positioned as a focused forensic-style review service for contested recordings. Email TeamMohamed@proton.me with a short case brief, the recording details, and the delivery timeline you need so scope and turnaround can be confirmed quickly.

Standard review

3 to 5 business days

Suitable for most first engagements where the matter needs careful review without emergency handling.

Urgent review

24 to 48 hours

Reserved for matters with a genuine deadline where the scope is practical and the evidence can be turned quickly.

Software path

Same day

Best for repeat users who already understand the workflow and want direct local execution after an initial engagement.

Case brief

Include the details that shape scope and quote.

  • What is the recording and why is it disputed?
  • What outcome are you trying to clarify?
  • Is there a specific timestamp or event of concern?
  • What is the file format, duration, and size?
  • What is your desired delivery timeline?

Delivered findings

A package built for review and handoff.

  • Executive summary of the review outcome
  • Ranked anomaly windows with timestamps
  • Short explanation of why each window matters
  • Supporting evidence bundle with artifacts and logs
  • Machine-readable findings package when needed

Review engagement

How a matter moves from intake to delivered findings.

The final delivery window depends on file size, complexity, urgency, and required review depth. The engagement flow stays consistent even when the schedule changes.

1

Case brief received

Share the recording details, dispute context, and any timestamps or events already under question.

2

Scope and schedule confirmed

Fit, urgency, and complexity are checked before quote, delivery window, and review depth are confirmed.

3

Analysis and analyst review

The recording goes through the selected workflow and the output is reviewed before delivery readiness is signed off.

4

Evidence bundle assembled

Summary, findings, artifacts, fingerprints, and checksums are assembled into the official result set.

5

Findings delivered

The client receives a repeatable, review-ready package that can be read without digging through raw JSON.
