Typical matters

Examples of disputes that fit this review path.

  • Police misconduct footage, arrest video disputes, or body-worn and custody-suite CCTV challenges.
  • Civil claims against police forces, councils, ministries, prisons, or other public bodies where recordings are central.
  • Corporate workplace incidents, misconduct allegations, site access disputes, and internal investigation evidence packs.
  • Insurer fraud review, claims disputes, staged-event concerns, and contested incident recordings.
  • Journalistic, NGO, or public-inquiry matters where a reviewable chain of reasoning is needed.

What drives price

Scope changes more with dispute pressure than with raw runtime.

  • Urgency: injunctions, hearings, executive deadlines, and disclosure timetables compress the delivery window.
  • Complexity: multiple clips, unclear provenance, cross-checking needs, and deeper reporting increase review depth.
  • Stake: matters touching litigation, public scrutiny, discipline, or reputation usually need a stronger handoff package.
  • Context: better case briefing and clearer timestamps reduce friction and improve turnaround.
  • Repeat use: once the workflow is understood, Investigator Edition becomes the lower-friction follow-on option.

Delivery flow

How the review moves from intake to bundle delivery.

The key timing variables are urgency, dispute pressure, file complexity, and required review depth. Those affect quote and delivery window more than the software license itself.

1

Submit case brief

Provide a short description of the recording, what is disputed, who is involved, and the desired outcome.

2

Confirm scope and urgency

Fit, delivery speed, review depth, and handoff expectations are checked before the final quote and window are confirmed.

3

Run and review analysis

The recording is processed through the selected workflow and the resulting review points are checked before the package is finalized.

4

Assemble official bundle

Summary, findings, artifacts, fingerprints, and checksums are packaged into the delivery set for counsel, decision-makers, or investigators.

5

Deliver review package

The client receives a structured package designed for actual handoff, not just an internal test result or black-box score.
